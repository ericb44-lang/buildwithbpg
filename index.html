WHO WE ARE

BPG, LLC brings many years of combined, hands-on experience to the General Contracting industry.

We are based in Northern Utah, and we work closely with owners who want clear direction, steady leadership, and someone in their corner from start to finish for their construction project.

Our background spans building high-end custom homes, managing complex remodels, and leading restoration projects. We understand local trades, inspectors, timelines, and the realities of building in this region.

Our Project Managers have led hundreds of projects from small, highly personal remodels to large custom homes and complicated rebuilds. That experience shows up in the details, the decisions, and the way problems get handled before they become expensive.

BPG, LLC is a licensed General Contractor in the state of Utah and fully insured.
